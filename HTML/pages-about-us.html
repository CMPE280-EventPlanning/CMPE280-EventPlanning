About Us

We are students of San Jose State University who are pursuing our Master's Degree.

Event planning has been carried out from many years starting from home to professional companies. The major complaint most of our customers was that there was no one single portal to view event locations remotely from the comfort of our home, office, etc. So we have come up with a unique idea of giving you a sneak preview of all the locations we provide before you visit them. This would help you to decide if the locations are suitable to your needs and also get you prepared regarding all the facilities and amenities provided. Whether it is a wedding, birthday party, business conference, student symposiums we strive to make your event a beautiful and a successful one.

  • Beautiful locations.
  • Virtual tour of all the locations we offer.
  • Amazing amenities and facilities.
  • Easy and fast booking of locations.
